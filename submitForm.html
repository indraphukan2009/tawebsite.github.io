Course Description:

This class does not have a specified curriculum. A student's grade in this class is dependent upon attendance, helpfulness, reliability, efficiency, dependability, and the quality of work. An above average grade is not automatic, it must be earned.

Behavior Expectations:

Students who engage in any activity considered dishonest, cheating, accessing unauthorized informtation, or a violation of school policies will be referred to the administration for disciplinary action and be indefinitely removed from the Teacher of Office Aide program.

It is expected that students will behave in a mature, professional manner during the time they are working as a Teacher or Office Aide. Students are expected to follow through promptly and efficiently with assigned duties, be honest and trustworthy, and refrain from passing on or discussing privileged information. Studenst will not wander the halls, goof-off or sit in the quad during the period assigned as an Aide. Use of any unauthorized electronic devices, excessive talking, or sleeping is not allowed and school dress codes are expected to be followed. Students are not permitted to eat in the classrooms or offices during class time.

I/We understand that any abuse of privilege of being in this class will lead to the student being removed from the class
