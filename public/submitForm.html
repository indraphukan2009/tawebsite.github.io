Teacher Aide Agreement Form

Course Description

This class does not have a specified curriculum. A student's grade in this class is dependent upon attendance, helpfulness, reliability, efficiency, dependability, and the quality of work. An above average grade is not automatic; it must be earned.

Behavior Expectations

Students are expected to behave in a mature, professional manner during their assigned time as a Teacher or Office Aide. They must follow through promptly with assigned duties, be honest and trustworthy, and refrain from discussing privileged information. Unauthorized phone use, excessive talking, or loitering are not allowed. Dress codes must be followed at all times.

I/We understand that any abuse of privilege of being in this class will lead to removal from the program.
